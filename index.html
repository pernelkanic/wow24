What ?

War of Words (WoW) is the inter-collegiate annual literary fest of Rajalakshmi Engineering College organized jointly by REC Atrium, the literary club of the college, and REC Debating Union, the debating society of the college. WoW’24, an extravaganza that celebrates English literature and creativity, will provide a platform for students across disciplines to exhibit their oratory skills, debating flair, and literary prowess through numerous events. The event WoW’24 is planned to be conducted on 26th February 2024.

Where ?

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Rajalakshmi Nagar, Thandalam, Chennai - 602 105.

When ?

Monday, 26th February, 2024

WoW Debate

A Clash of words

WoW Debate allows teams to take part in a Parliamentary debate and use their wit and persuasiveness to battle it out and convince the judges that they are right.



Rules:

1. This is a team event with 2 participants per team.

2. The motion for the debate will be provided on spot, along with a brief preparation time. For & against the motion will be randomly assigned.

3. General Parliamentary debate procedures will be followed, with each speaker's timing being 4 minutes.

4. The format will primarily be British Parliamentary.

5. Use of unparliamentary language and vulgarity is strictly prohibited.

6. The Judge’s decision will be final.

Treasure Among Us

Chase the Trail.

Treasure Among Us offers two captivating rounds of mystery and strategy. In the first round, participants tackle tasks while impostors lurk, aiming to outwit and eliminate them. Winners advance to a high-stakes treasure hunt, racing to find the key to the ultimate battle royale. It's an adrenaline-fueled journey of camaraderie and triumph, where every victory is a testament to courage and cunning.



Rules:

1. This is an individual event.

2. The participants will have to complete tasks to decode clues to find the treasure.

3. The imposter will act as a barricade.

4. Profanity of any kind will lead to disqualification.

5. The participants will be judged based on their rapid proceedings, and ability to decode and identify the imposter.

6. Best three players will move on to the final round and battle out to find the hidden treasure.

Lit-Quiz

The court is now in session.

An evaluation of one's literary acumen, individual participants will face questions encompassing both contemporary and timeless works of literature.



Rules:

1. The event will take place in 2 rounds, with questions based on classic and contemporary novels, authors and poems.

2. The first round will be a Pen-and-Paper style Multiple Choice Quiz where the questions are read out by the Organising Committee and the participants have to write down the answers in a clear, legible handwriting.

3. There will be 20 questions in the first round, with each question carrying 10 points and the participants will have to secure at least 100 points in the first round to qualify for the second round.

4. Usage of any electronic devices and aids are prohibited. Smartphones and other paraphernalia must be submitted with the Organising Committee before commencement of the event.The second round will be a buzzer-round where the questions will be read out to the participants and the participants have to sound the buzzer if they know the answer. The order in which the participants are called to present their answer will be the order in which the buzzer was hit, if the first participant to sound the buzzer fails to provide the correct answer.

5.There will be 10 questions in the second round, with 10 points for the correct answer.

6. The participants with the highest scores at the end of the second round will be named as the winners of the LitQuiz.

7. The decision of the Quizmaster is final.

Lawyer Up

The court is now in session.

In this solo challenge, participants will be presented with the intricacies of a crime scene. Thinking swiftly on their feet, they must adeptly advocate for their client's innocence, crafting a compelling defense strategy.



Rules:

1. In this event, two participants will be given a case file in which the crime scene and forensic details will be described. Each person will be assigned a suspect, and your task will be to act as a lawyer and try to prove your client's innocence to the jury within 2.5 minutes each (1.5 minutes + 1 minute).

2. Each participant will be judged based on eloquence, persuasiveness, relevance, creativity, and the use of legal jargon.

3. New witnesses cannot be introduced by the lawyers.

4. The judge can bring in new characters, evidence, or change facts to stress test the lawyers.

5. The usage of slurs, being disrespectful towards the participant will not be tolerated.

Shipwreck

Clutch the lifejacket.

Shipwreck is a game where players, as shipwreck survivors, strategically persuade the captain for a life jacket to escape a sinking ship, managing alliances and conflicting motives.



Rules:

1. This is an individual event.

2. Each character is assigned on the spot by the judges.

3. Each participant will get a maximum of 5 minutes for their speech.

4. Participants will be asked to switch between Shipwreck and Anti-shipwreck.

5. Judges may ask relevant questions during the participant’s speech or after.

6. Participants are required to behave with the utmost decorum, and any unparliamentary words and vulgarity are strictly prohibited.

7. The judge’s decision is final.

Prompt-Verse

The court is now in session.

In this immersive challenge, participants delve into the intricate art of storytelling, seamlessly melding together vital elements. Their task involves crafting a narrative using provided prompts and masterfully narrating their tale. The evaluation focuses on their literary finesse and their skill in captivating and engaging the audience. Success hinges not only on the written word but also on the participants' ability to craft an enthralling oral presentation.



Rules:

1. A Prompt will be provided on the spot, post which all the participants will be allotted 40 minutes to pen down a story following the prompt.

2. The story cannot exceed a word count of 300 words.

3. Usage of any electronic devices and aids are prohibited. Smartphones and other paraphernalia must be submitted with the Organising Committee before commencement of the event.

4. After the allotted 40 minutes have elapsed, the participants will be called up to the stage by the Organising Committee, upon which they have to present their story in an engaging manner.

5. The judging criteria include creativity, plot structure, originality, adherence to the prompt and presentation style.

6. It is enforced that the participants not tamper with their written pieces after the allotted time for writing has elapsed.

Mystery Rooms

The court is now in session.

A mystery room is a captivating mini literature room where knowledge and creativity intertwine. Solve puzzles inspired by novels, decipher coded messages from literary quotes, a mini-game, and unlock hidden compartments filled with clues. Immerse yourself in the world of words as you connect the dots between stories to progress through levels. Only those who unravel the literary enigmas will emerge victorious from this unique and engaging physical escape challenge.



Rules:

1. This is a team event, and each team can consist of 2-4 members.

2. All participants are required to maintain decorum throughout the event.

3. Teams must complete tasks using hints provided within the room. Each team can request a maximum of 1 clue per level. Points will be awarded for each completed task.

4. Tasks may involve decoding ciphers, solving riddles, analyzing character motivations, and piecing together storylines provided.

5. This will be a timed event.

6. Teams must complete each given room before proceeding to the next. Teams are allowed to revisit previous levels if necessary.

Adzap

The court is now in session.

Adzap is an event where participants embark on transformative journeys of brand evolution and narrative discovery. Script-writing

The court is now in session.

In this creative endeavor, participants will receive a curated selection of established fictional stories. Their imaginative challenge is to craft a unique conclusion for any chosen narrative and subsequently pen a script for this alternative ending. The emphasis is on unleashing their creativity to reshape the storyline, offering a fresh perspective and narrative twist to captivate and surprise their audience.



Rules:

1. Scripts must be written within a word limit of 1500 words.

2. Profanity is strictly prohibited in all scripts.

3. Authors are required to adhere to the rule of script writing.

4. Compliance with these guidelines is mandatory throughout the scripting process.

Poetry

The court is now in session.

In this innovative challenge, candidates are empowered to select a beloved fictional character and address a modern-day issue in the real world of their choosing. The task encourages participants to channel their creativity and problem-solving skills, using the unique traits of their chosen character to propose inventive solutions to present-day challenges.



Rules:

1. Engage in the enchanting task of crafting a poem.

2. There's no strict word limit, but keep it concise, under 1500 words.

3. Ensure your verses remain pure, free from any offensive language.

4. Embrace the beauty of poetry as you weave your lines, adhering to these guidelines throughout your creative journey.
